---
description: Perform a tiered corporate audit of the current codebase.
---

1. Call the **President** skill to define the audit parameters and vision.
2. Call the **VP of Engineering** and **VP of AI** to identify high-level bottlenecks.
3. Call the **Directors** (Core, UX, AI Arch) to perform deep-dive inspections of their domains.
4. Call the **Managers** and **Employees** to document specific bugs or technical debt.
5. Call the **President QA** to synthesize all findings into a "State of the Project" report.
6. Present the report via `notify_user` with a "Strategic Remediation Plan."
